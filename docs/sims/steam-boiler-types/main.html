Steam Boiler Types Comparison

Firetube Boiler

Scotch Marine
Principle: Hot gases inside tubes, water outside
Capacity: Up to ~800 HP
Pressure: Up to 250 psig
Efficiency: 80–85%
Best for: Building heating, food processing, small-medium industrial

Watertube Boiler

High Pressure
Principle: Water inside tubes, hot gases outside
Capacity: 10–1,000+ MMBtu/hr
Pressure: Up to 3,000+ psig
Efficiency: 80–88%
Best for: Power plants, refineries, large industrial

Electric Boiler

Zero Emissions
Principle: Electrode or resistance heating
Capacity: Up to ~100 MMBtu/hr
Pressure: Up to 250 psig
Efficiency: 98–99%
Best for: Clean environments, emission-restricted areas

Condensing Boiler

High Efficiency
Principle: Flue gas latent heat recovery
Capacity: Up to ~20 MMBtu/hr
Pressure: Typically low pressure
Efficiency: 95–98%
Best for: Low-temp hot water, hydronic heating
