## About this MicroSim

This interactive visual checklist covers five maintenance areas for vacuum systems: seals and connections, vacuum pump, filters and traps, gauges and instruments, and piping and receivers. Technicians can check off completed tasks and track their progress through a systematic inspection.

## Learning Objective

Students will be able to perform a systematic vacuum system maintenance inspection by following a structured checklist for seals, pumps, filters, gauges, and piping.
