# Sustainability Metrics Calculator

This interactive MicroSim helps students calculate and evaluate key sustainability metrics for fluid power facilities. Enter energy, water, and emissions data to compute Energy Use Intensity (EUI), Water Use Intensity (WUI), and carbon footprint. Compare results against industry benchmarks and explore what-if scenarios for improvement.
