## About this MicroSim

This interactive maintenance checklist covers the four critical areas of HVAC maintenance: filters, coils, ductwork, and condensate management. Click items to mark them complete and track your progress through a systematic inspection.

## Learning Objective

Students will be able to perform a systematic HVAC maintenance inspection by following a structured checklist for filters, coils, ducts, and condensate management.
