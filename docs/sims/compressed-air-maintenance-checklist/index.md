## About this MicroSim

This interactive checklist guides technicians through a systematic compressed air system maintenance inspection, covering compressors, dryers, filters, distribution piping, and receiver tanks with progress tracking.

## Learning Objective

Students will be able to perform a systematic compressed air system maintenance inspection by following a structured checklist for compressors, dryers, filters, piping, and drains.
