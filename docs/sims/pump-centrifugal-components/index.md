## About this MicroSim

This MicroSim provides an interactive cross-section cutaway view of a centrifugal pump. Hover over any component to see its name and a brief description. Click on a component to view detailed information in the side panel.

## Learning Objective

Students will be able to identify and label the major components of a centrifugal pump, including the impeller, volute casing, shaft, mechanical seal, wear rings, bearings, suction eye, discharge port, and motor coupling.
